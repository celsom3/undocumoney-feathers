The Campaign

Here's how it works...

The Economy Needs Us

The U.S. ecomony depends on the money that goes through undocumented immigrants. We buy things.

How Much Money?

To prove the inextricable value and nature of undocumented immigrants, we created #UndocuMoney.

#UndocuMoney

Every dollar bill that a participating undocumented person handles will have the hashtag #Undocumoney written on it and be posted to social media.

The Creators

Meet the team behind the hashtag

Marisol Soto

Creator

The idea of Undocumoney was the idea of Marisol's. She hopes that through this campaign we can educate and show people the real contributions undocumented immigrants like her and her family make to the economy. We help make this country a better place.

Andrew Jonhson

Videographer, Photographer/Editor

As an allie for the DREAM movement, Andrew showcases his talents through his video art and hopes that one day we can mend our broken immigration system.

Irvin Camacho

Script Writer, Creative Director

Irvin believes no human being is illegal and undocumented students and hard working parents deserve a chance to pursue the American Dream

Celso Mireles

Web Developer

Undocumented and Unafraid, he wants to create technology for social justice. He makes websites and helps people movements build the right digital tools for the job.
